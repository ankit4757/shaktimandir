Beginner Exercises

Jumping Jacks

("उच्चकूदकः शरीरं त्वरितं करोति।")

Great warm-up to get your heart rate up and prepare your body for workout.

Wall Sit

("दीवारसंस्थापनं जंघायाः दृढतां वर्धयति।")

Strengthens thighs and improves lower body endurance using body weight.

Crunches

("उदरमांसपेशीं शक्तिं ददाति।")

A simple core exercise to strengthen abdominal muscles and improve posture.

Knee Push-ups

("जंघासमर्पितं दण्डवदनं ऊर्ध्वशरीरबलं वर्धयति।")

A lighter version of push-ups for building upper body strength at the beginner level.

High Knee Marching

("ऊर्ध्वजङ्घायाः पदविन्यासः सन्तुलनं च समन्वयशक्तिं च वर्धयति।")

Gentle cardio that helps improve balance, coordination, and lower body strength.

Arm Circles

("बाहुचक्रसञ्चलनं स्कन्धमांसपेशीं सक्रियं करोति।")

A light warm-up exercise to activate shoulder joints and upper body muscles.
