Types of Exercises

Push Ups

("दण्डवदनं वक्षःस्थलं, बाहुयोः च बलं वर्धयति।")

A basic upper body strength exercise that targets the chest, shoulders, and triceps.

Squats

("उपवेशनं जंघायाः शक्तिं पृष्ठबलं च वर्धयति।")

Excellent for strengthening your legs and glutes while improving posture.

Plank

("फलकासनं उदरस्थैर्यं च धैर्यं च वर्धयति।")

Builds core stability and endurance. Great for abs and posture.

Lunges

("विन्यासः समत्वं च जंघाशक्तिं च वर्धयति।")

Improves balance, coordination, and leg strength.

Deadlifts

("मृतभारवहनं पूर्णशरीरबलं वर्धयति।")

Powerful full-body movement that builds strength in legs, back, and core.

Pull-Ups

("आकर्षणं पृष्ठं, बाहुशक्तिं च विकासयति।")

Targets the upper body including lats, biceps, and shoulders.

Bench Press

("उत्तानासनं वक्षोबलं बाहुबलं च वर्धयति।")

Classic chest-building movement that also works shoulders and triceps.

Crunches

("उदरमांसपेशीं दृढां करोति।")

A simple yet effective abdominal workout to tone your core.

Burpees

("पूर्णशरीरस्य व्यायामः शक्तिं सह धैर्यं च वर्धयति।")

Full-body cardio move that builds strength and endurance.

Jumping Jacks

("उच्चकूदकः रक्तप्रवाहं च धैर्यं च वर्धयति।")

Cardio warm-up that improves circulation and gets your heart rate up.
