Advanced Gym Exercises

Barbell Squat

("भारभारकउपवेशनं जंघाशक्तिं पृष्ठं च बलयति।")

A compound powerhouse for legs, glutes, and core. Perfect for building lower body strength.

Barbell Bench Press

("वक्षोबलवर्धकं व्यायामं त्रिकोनमांसपेशीं च उद्दीपयति।")

Essential chest builder. Targets pectorals, triceps, and front delts with heavy resistance.

Deadlift

("पूर्णशरीरव्यायामः जंघा, पृष्ठं, हस्तग्रहम् च शक्तिं वर्धयति।")

Total-body movement focusing on hamstrings, glutes, back, and grip strength.

Military Press

("शिरोपरि भारोत्थापनं बाहुबलं स्कन्धस्थैर्यं च विकासयति।")

Classic shoulder builder. Increases upper body strength and stability in overhead lifts.

Lat Pulldown

("पृष्ठमांसपेशीं विस्तारयति, उर्ध्वाङ्गबलं च ददाति।")

Engages the lats and upper back. Great for building width and pull-up strength.

Leg Press

("जंघायाः पृथक् अभ्यासः नितम्बमांसपेशीं च दृढयति।")

Isolated lower body workout to safely target quads, hamstrings, and glutes with heavy loads.

Tricep Rope Pushdown

("त्रिपृष्ठमांसपेशीवर्धनार्थं श्रेष्ठः पृथकव्यायामः।")

Isolation move for building stronger, defined triceps. Best performed with proper form.

Cable Crossover

("वक्षोस्थलरूपविकासार्थं गतिसमन्वितं व्यायामः।")

Excellent for chest shaping and upper pecs. Allows full range of motion under control.

Seated Cable Row

("मध्यपृष्ठं दृढयति, उत्तमं स्थितिसन्तुलनं वर्धयति।")

Improves posture and targets middle back muscles, including rhomboids and lats.
